Who we are:

Tokyo Taste offers delicious dining and takeout to Elmhurst, NY.

Tokyo Taste is a cornerstone in the Elmhurst community and has been recognized for its outstanding Sushi cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.

Our Sushi restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
